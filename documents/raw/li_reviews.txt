Kai Li's lectures are highly structured and content-rich. Many students report that her classes move at a steady pace, with a strong focus on research examples and real-world applications. While the lectures are clear, the material can be dense, so students are encouraged to review notes immediately after class.

The homework is demanding and often requires applying theory to new problems. Reviewers say that the assignments are useful for learning the material, but they can be time-consuming, especially when the problems require careful reasoning.

Students generally find Kai Li accessible, but some noted that office hours are limited and appointments fill quickly. Overall, the course is rewarding for those who enjoy combining theory and application, but it requires disciplined study habits.

Exam questions were described as fair but broad, incorporating both conceptual understanding and problem-solving. A few reviewers recommended practicing with past problem sets and seeking help early if a concept is unclear.
